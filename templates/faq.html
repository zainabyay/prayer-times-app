Adding to your calendar

Google Calendar subscriptions can only be set up on desktop. Go to calendar.google.com, click the + next to "Other calendars" in the left sidebar, select "From URL", paste your link and click Add. Once added it will also appear in the Google Calendar app on your phone automatically.
You can add it on iPhone or Mac.
  1. iPhone: Go to Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar, then paste your link.
  2. Mac: Open Calendar, go to File → New Calendar Subscription, and paste your link.
Note: the iCloud web version on Windows does not support calendar subscriptions. Once added via iCloud it syncs across all your Apple devices.
Outlook subscriptions must be set up on desktop or web — the mobile app does not support adding new subscriptions directly. Go to outlook.com or Outlook desktop, click Add Calendar → Subscribe from web, and paste your link. Once added it will appear in the Outlook mobile app on your phone automatically.

Reminders

If you have reminders turned on when generating your link, a 10 minute alert is built into each prayer event. This works reliably on Apple Calendar and Outlook. Google Calendar on desktop does not support alerts from calendar subscriptions, but you may still receive notifications via the Google Calendar mobile app depending on your device settings.
Google Calendar desktop ignores alerts set in external calendar subscriptions — this is a Google limitation and affects all similar tools, not just ours. For the most reliable notifications we recommend using Apple Calendar or Outlook, or using a dedicated prayer app like Pillars alongside this tool.

Privacy & data

No personal data is stored. We don't ask for your name, email, or any account details. Your city name is used to calculate prayer times and is temporarily cached to keep the app fast, but it is not linked to you in any way.
Your link still works — it lives on our server, not in your browser. If you lose it, just go back to the app, enter the same city, and generate a new one. Any previously added calendar subscriptions using your old link will continue to work fine.
